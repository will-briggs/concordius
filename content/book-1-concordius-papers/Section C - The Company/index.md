# Section C — The Company

*Papers C0–C7*

---

The structure was carried, in whole or in part, by particular people long before it was named. Section C is the **Mi** of the larger octave — the inspirations: the lives, texts, and works in which the Good, the True, and the Beautiful, and the courage, temperance, and diligence that hold them, were lived out and handed forward. It is built as a test of the holographic principle. Each note takes one of the seven elements of Cl(3,0) and reads it across **three witnesses who could not have arranged their agreement** — kept deliberately wide across the breadth of human production: philosophy and sacred text, painting and architecture, mathematics and natural science, poetry and film and music, a life and a contemporary saying. When channels that share no source converge on one structure, the convergence is the evidence (Feature 6). The notes gather, at the Si, into the Host of Witnesses — the cloud the reader joins at the Crossing — and the section ends by turning to face the inversion of all of it.
