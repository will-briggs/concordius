# Section C — The Company

*Papers C0–C7*

---

The structure was carried, in whole or in part, by particular people long before it was named. Section C is the **Mi** of the larger octave — the inspirations: the lives in which the Good, the True, and the Beautiful were lived out and handed forward. It moves through the great readers and makers — Plato, Augustine, Aristotle; Joan, Bach, Franklin — and gathers them into the Host of Witnesses, the cloud whose company the reader joins. It is a crossing made together; and it ends by turning to face the thing the witnesses stand against — the Inversion — which is the hinge into the section that follows.
