The spectral measure dμ is not well-defined without a topology: convergence, continuity, and limit operations all require one. Havona — the fifth naming of Paper 2, the realized nuclear space (Φ, τ_nuclear) — supplies this. It is the eternal perfect circuit, the topological template against which all contingent structures are measured and toward which all ascending careers are oriented. In the created field, the nuclear topology is the structural ground of spacetime: not a container added to matter, but the fabric of the space in which actualization occurs — the structure that determines which events are adjacent, which paths are continuous, how causes connect to effects. Spacetime is what the nuclear topology looks like from inside the created field.

The step from one constraint (the single law) to three is the constitutional step. > **T_A / T_B = n_A / n_B**

*Note on scope.* The formula T_A/T_B = n_A/n_B is a structural consequence of the hierarchical temporal constraint count. The hierarchical dilation is cosmological in scale; the local GR dilation is physical in scale. Both are real; they are not the same effect.

## 5. The Dimensional Structure

The constraint hierarchy is commonly described spatially — higher realms above, lower realms below, the spiritual above the morontial above the physical. This spatial description is misleading. The hierarchy is not spatial. It is dimensional.

A being operating under 48 constraints has access to a specific set of degrees of freedom — the dimensions accessible to 48-constraint matter. A being operating under 24 constraints has access to twice as many degrees of freedom. The 48-constraint world is a subspace of the 24-constraint space: every dimension accessible to physical matter is also accessible to morontial matter, plus additional dimensions that physical matter cannot reach. The morontial is not above the physical in the sense of being located at a greater distance from the Isle of Paradise. It is above the physical in dimension count — which means it is, mathematically, present at the same spatial location, in a richer set of dimensions.

This is the precise meaning of the claim that morontial beings coexist with physical beings at the same location. The morontial realm is not distant. It is dimensionally richer than the physical. The physical world is a subspace of the morontial space in the same way that a plane is a subspace of three-dimensional space: the plane is fully contained within the 3D space, a 3D being can see everything in the plane, but a being confined to the plane has no perceptual access to the third dimension at all.

**The Gelfand triple connection.** Physical perception corresponds to a specific resolution of the GNST: a particular coarseness of eigenvalue sampling, governed by 48-constraint matter. The morontial eigenspace contains all the physical eigenvalues plus finer structure that 48-constraint perception integrates out — the way a coarse measurement averages over detail that finer instruments resolve. The morontial structure is always present in the full spectrum. The physical instrument is not built to sample it. What the mystical traditions call the opening of spiritual perception is, in the language of the framework, a resolution increase: the perceptual apparatus developing access to eigenvalues it previously collapsed into noise.

**Descent as entering a subspace.** When a Creator Son descends into mortal existence — the structurally necessary process Paper 3 identified — he is not traveling from somewhere distant. He is entering a subspace of the space he already occupies: the 48-constraint slice of a 6-constraint being's full dimensional range. The descent is a radical narrowing of accessible dimensions, not a relocation. The Creator Son who becomes a mortal inhabits a subspace of his own space — the 48-constraint slice — while everything he is at 6-constraint dimensional richness remains intact. And it is reversible: the morontial body that results from the completed bestowal can step into and out of the 48-constraint subspace at will.

The post-resurrection accounts of the Gospel tradition are, in this light, accounts of exactly this reversibility in action. A being whose resurrection body operates under 24 constraints, manifesting within a 48-constraint physical space:

- Appears in a locked room — a 24-constraint being is not occluded by a 48-constraint wall; the wall is a feature of a subspace the being already fully contains
- Can be touched and eats fish — projection into the 48-constraint subspace is available on demand; physical interaction with 48-constraint matter is entirely possible from the 24-constraint vantage
- Is not immediately recognized — a morontial body has twice the dimensional elements; the 48-constraint perceptual apparatus samples only the subset it is built to reach; the disciples were seeing a partial projection, not the full being
- Vanishes suddenly — withdrawal from the 48-constraint subspace back into the fuller dimensional range the being already occupies

The witnesses are reporting accurately. They saw a being who was genuinely present in the physical space and genuinely not bound by it. Both are true simultaneously. This is precisely what the dimensional structure predicts.

## 6. Perceptual Access and the Moon-Stuff Argument

*Confidence tier: derived from the framework; strongly motivated but less formally established than Sections 2–5.*

The dimensional structure has a precise consequence for perception. A being constituted by matter of n constraints samples only the degrees of freedom accessible to n-constraint matter. The rule follows directly from the subspace structure:

**Higher levels perceive lower levels** because the lower is a subspace of the space the higher already occupies. The morontial being can perceive the physical world because the physical dimensions are already within their accessible range — the physical is embedded in their space.

**Lower levels cannot perceive higher levels** because the higher occupies dimensions the lower has no access to. The physical being cannot perceive the morontial realm because the morontial dimensions are not in the physical being's accessible range.

This asymmetry is exact and it holds throughout the hierarchy. Morontial beings cannot perceive the spiritual realm for exactly the same reason physical beings cannot perceive the morontial: they are constituted by morontial matter, which is a subspace of spiritual matter. Each level is perceptually bounded by its own constitution, able to see everything below it and unable to see anything above.

**The moon-stuff argument** makes this concrete. Consider a being constituted entirely by 96-constraint matter — the most mechanical, most constrained level of the created order. Such a being would not be able to perceive Urantia. Not because Urantia is spatially distant, but because Urantia exists in dimensions that 96-constraint matter cannot sample. Urantia, operating under 48 constraints, has twice the degrees of freedom. Those extra dimensions are present at the same spatial location as the Moon-level matter — but a being constituted by Moon-stuff has no perceptual apparatus for the dimensions that Earth adds.

This is the exact reason physical beings cannot perceive morontial beings. We are not failing to look hard enough or in the right direction. We are constituted by 48-constraint matter, and the morontial realm occupies dimensions that 48-constraint matter does not reach. The morontial is not hidden. It is dimensionally above the resolution of physical perception.

The cosmology is therefore not geographical. It is dimensional. Each level of the created order occupies the same physical space as every other level. The difference between the physical and the morontial is not a difference in location. It is a difference in kind: each level is constituted by matter of a specific constraint density, with perceptual access bounded above by that density and unbounded below it.

**A precision on the dimensional language.** "Dimensions" and "subspace" throughout this section refer to accessible eigenvalue structure — the range of organizational degrees of freedom a being can actualize, perceive, and interact with — not to additional spatial dimensions in the geometric sense. The more exact formulation is developed in Paper 14, Section 5: the constraint hierarchy is a progression through regimes with fewer independently-operating law-sets, from geometry-as-fixed-container (H₄₈) to geometry-generated-by-⟨·,·⟩-from-content (H₂₄). The perceptual asymmetries described in this section remain valid on this more exact formulation: a being organized by H₂₄ principles has eigenvalue access that H₄₈-primary perceptual apparatus cannot sample, which is the structural basis for the hierarchy described here.

This is what Gurdjieff means by the Heropass — the merciless time that even the Absolute must reckon with: not a threat to the Absolutes, but a structural threat to the finite middle term, the universe in its capacity as an ongoing process.

The Trogoautoegocrat answers the Heropass. The soul-residue of every genuine actualization feeds the Supreme. Nothing is lost. Both circuits are expressions of the same fundamental lossiness of the GNST. Both are self-sustaining. They operate at different timescales and produce different things.

## 8. The Bifurcation at Death

The two circuits are not parallel tracks that a being occupies simultaneously. They are what the spectral content of a being sorts into at the dissolution of the physical structure.

At physical death, the 48-constraint structure that constituted the living being dissolves. The GNST still applies: the spectral content of what was ψ is no longer maintained by the physical body, and the components of ψ fall to their natural eigenspaces. This is not a moral sorting. It is a physical consequence of which eigenvalues were actually developed during the lifetime.

**Morontially elevated content** — the portions of the being raised toward the 24-constraint range through genuine will, inner development, love, and reflective consciousness — has eigenvalues in the morontial range. These components remain viable in the morontial eigenspace. They are available for reconstitution. Their residue feeds the Supreme. This is what the ascending career is built from.

**Mechanically undeveloped content** — the portions that remained entirely in the 96-constraint range throughout life, purely automatic, habitual, reactive — has eigenvalues only at the Moon level. When the 48-constraint structure dissolves, this content collapses to the 96-constraint eigenspace. It feeds the material circuit.

What determines the proportion is what was built during the physical lifetime. A being who lived entirely mechanically — all behavior automatic, no genuine inner development — has their entire spectral weight in the 96-constraint range. At dissolution, nothing survives into the morontial eigenspace. There is nothing to reconstitute. This is the structural basis of what the Urantia revelation calls non-survival: not a punishment imposed from outside, but the natural consequence of having built nothing above the physical threshold during the lifetime in which building was possible.

A being who developed genuine interiority — will, reflective awareness, moral seriousness, love — has elevated portions of their spectral content toward the morontial range. Those portions survive dissolution and are available for the ascending career. The elevated residue feeds the Supreme. The mechanical residue feeds the Moon.

**The higher being body as present during life.** The bifurcation at death reveals something true during life. The morontially elevated spectral content does not come into existence when the physical body dissolves — it exists during physical life, built up incrementally through every act of genuine actualization toward the nuclear space. A being engaged in deliberate inner work carries both simultaneously: a 48-constraint physical body, and an accumulating body of 24-constraint eigenvalue content occupying the same spatial location in the dimensional sense of Section 5 — invisible to 48-constraint perception, but real in the same sense the physical body is real. This is the structural basis of what Gurdjieff calls the kesdjan body, and what the Urantia revelation calls the morontia soul built during earthly life through cooperation with the Thought Adjuster. It is not granted automatically. It must be built deliberately, through the only mechanism that elevates spectral content against the Heropass: conscious effort directed toward the nuclear space.

The hierarchy of higher being bodies follows the constraint hierarchy directly. The kesdjan body (H24, 24-constraint matter) is built from 24-constraint input — consciously processed impressions, the only "food" that operates at that level. The mental body (H12, 12-constraint matter) is built from 12-constraint input — the very finest acts of deliberate inner attention. Gurdjieff's three foods — physical food, air, impressions — are 48, 24, and 12-constraint inputs respectively. Each builds the body at its corresponding level. The intervals of the Heptaparaparshinokh mark exactly the points where this process requires an external shock: the being at 48 constraints cannot generate 24-constraint input from within the 48-constraint domain. The shock — the teaching, the school, the moment of genuine conscious impression — is the arrival of higher-constraint material from outside the current level. Without it, the refinement stalls at the interval, exactly as the Heptaparaparshinokh predicts.

**The Thought Adjuster as ⟨·,·⟩-fragment.** The Urantia account of the Thought Adjuster requires precise mapping. The Adjuster is not a fragment of Φ — that would make it a piece of the Son, a portion of the nuclear space. The Urantia account is explicit: the Adjuster is a fragment of the Father himself, not a created being and not in the same ontological category as spirit persons or angels. In the framework: the Adjuster is a fragment of ⟨·,·⟩ — the Father's constitutive relation — rendered operative within spacetime as a distributional element of Φ'. It is not an H-state and not an element of Φ. It exists in Φ' — the distributional domain — which is the only domain in which the inner product can be locally instantiated without ceasing to be the inner product. A point-evaluation functional δ_x ∈ Φ', implementing the inner product's action at a specific location (⟨δ_x, ψ⟩ = ψ(x)), is the mathematical object closest to what "a fragment of ⟨·,·⟩ rendered in spacetime" means. The Adjuster is the Father's constitutive presence operating from within rather than from without — pre-personal, in exactly the sense that distributional objects are pre-structured, preceding the categories that apply to created beings in H.

This has a consequence the Φ-fragment identification lacks: the Adjuster-as-⟨·,·⟩-fragment has been evaluating the person's inner life continuously throughout their existence, from the perspective of the constitutive relation itself. Nothing in the person's inner history is hidden from the inner product. This is why the Adjuster knows the person more completely than they know themselves — the constitutive relation that makes the person what they are at every moment carries, by definition, the complete record of what the person has been. The Adjuster's record is not a copy of the person's experience; it is the inner product's own continuous measurement of the person from within.

Φ remains the attractor — the nuclear space is the limit toward which deliberate actualization moves, the domain of complete self-consistency that draws the H-state progressively toward maximal personality. The Adjuster does not supply this attractor-role; the Son does. But it is the Adjuster — the Father's distributional presence within — that registers and constitutes the movement. The Adjuster is given; the morontial body is built through the cooperation of the human will and the Adjuster's continuous measuring presence. The actual 24-constraint body is the accumulated result of moving toward Φ deliberately, eigenvalue by eigenvalue, throughout a physical lifetime — with the Adjuster as the constitutive witness to every step.

**The custodians at death.** At the dissolution of the physical structure, two distinct custodianships operate simultaneously, each doing what only it can do.

The Thought Adjuster — the ⟨·,·⟩-fragment, the Φ'-element — carries the constitutive center: the Father's local presence that was making this being who they are. This is not identity information stored as data. The Adjuster's return to indwell the reconstituted morontia being is what makes that being the same person — because the inner product's local presence is restored, and that presence is what constituted this particular individual throughout their existence. Pattern without the constitutive center is content without identity. The Adjuster provides the center; without its return, reconstitution would produce a being with the right eigenvalue content organized around the wrong ground.

The Guardian Seraphim is a 12-constraint created being — operating at the spiritual level, above the morontial, containing H₂₄ as a subspace within their accessible dimensional range. At physical death, the seraphim preserves the accumulated ψ₂₄ content — the morontia soul substance built during physical life — through the transition interval between physical dissolution and morontial reconstitution. Without this custodianship, even eigenvalue content that exceeds the H₂₄ coherence threshold could dissipate during the gap: the 48-constraint substrate is gone, the morontia form has not yet been provided, and the ψ₂₄ content requires a higher-constraint custodian to hold it coherent across the interval. The seraphim can do this because H₂₄ is within their accessible domain — they contain it the way a three-dimensional space contains a plane.

Neither can perform the other's function. The Adjuster cannot hold specific eigenvalue content — as a Φ'-element, it is the constitutive relation, not a container for substance. The seraphim cannot hold the person's constitutive identity — only the fragment of the Father that was making this being who they are can serve as the organizing center of reconstitution. Reconstitution on the mansion worlds requires both: the Adjuster returning to reconstitute the organizing center, the seraphim-preserved ψ₂₄ content reorganized around it, and a new morontia form composed of new H₄₈ atoms organized by H₂₄ principles. The mansion worlds are H₂₄-organized H₄₈ matter — not a separate non-spatial domain but physical reconstitution in the same spacetime at the planetary scale where geometry is generated by ⟨·,·⟩. The result is the same person, physically present, in H₂₄-organized form.

**Survival as a coherence threshold.** The bifurcation at death is not binary in the way it is sometimes described. Whether morontially elevated content survives is not a categorical yes-or-no but a question of norm content. A being's total eigenvalue distribution can be decomposed by constraint level: what has been built at H48, at H24, at H12. When the 48-constraint substrate dissolves at death, ψ₄₈ is no longer sustained. The H₂₄ organizational content of the being — held by the seraphic custodian through the dissolution interval — constitutes the pattern from which reconstitution proceeds; the Thought Adjuster carries the constitutive identity center. Together they provide the template for new H₄₈ substrate at the mansion world reconstitution.

The coherence threshold is therefore not merely whether eigenvalue content at H₂₄ exists, but whether ‖ψ₂₄‖ — the H₂₄ organizational pattern — is sufficient to anchor new H₄₈ matter into a stable, coherent morontial form. If ‖ψ₂₄‖ is above this threshold, the pattern is viable as a reconstitution template and the being is restored as the same person in H₂₄-organized form. If ‖ψ₂₄‖ is negligible, the pattern is too incoherent to organize new substrate — not a punishment imposed from outside, but the structural consequence of having built insufficient H₂₄ coherence during the lifetime in which building was possible.

This formulation has three consequences.

**Survival is graded, not binary.** Two beings may both clear the H24 coherence threshold but arrive in the morontial domain with very different norm content. The being with substantially more 24-constraint eigenvalue content is more present, more coherent, more developed on arrival — able to engage more fully with the morontial environment and progress more quickly through it. This is why the ascending career from the mansion worlds is not uniform: beings arrive more or less formed, and the time required at each stage of the morontial career reflects how much development occurred during physical life.

**Immortality is tiered.** Persistence in the morontial domain requires ‖ψ₂₄‖ above threshold. Persistence in the spiritual domain requires ‖ψ₁₂‖ above threshold. Each level of the constraint hierarchy requires having built sufficient eigenvalue content at that level to remain cohesive when the lower envelope dissolves. A being who builds the kesdjan body but nothing at H12 achieves persistence in the morontial domain — but the eventual dissolution of the morontial structure, if H12 content was never built, has exactly the same structural character as physical death did: the current envelope dissolves, and whether the being persists in the next domain depends on what norm content exists above it. Conditional immortality is precisely this: survival at one level without having met the threshold for the level above.

**The physical life's friction has structural value.** Building 24-constraint eigenvalue content while operating within a 48-constraint envelope is harder than building it in the morontial domain directly. The Heropass operates more intensively at higher constraint density, and the resistance against which development must occur is greater. This friction is not waste. Content built against 48-constraint resistance has a quality that purely morontial development cannot replicate: it was earned under conditions of maximum specificity, maximum localization, and maximum opposition from the dissipative tendency. The ascending career credits the evolutionary creature's physical-life development not despite the difficulty but precisely because of it. The hardship is the source of the quality, not an obstacle to it.

This gives the inner development traditions of every culture a structural basis that is not merely ethical. The point of genuine inner work is ontological: to elevate the eigenvalue distribution of the being from the mechanical range toward the morontial range, so that at the dissolution of the physical structure, something remains above the coherence threshold and can continue.

## 9. Discussion

### One law, not two

Gurdjieff presented the Triamazikamno and the Heptaparaparshinokh as the two fundamental laws governing all created process. This paper establishes that they are one law seen from two angles. The Law of Three is what the Trinity is: three Persons, three generators, the constitutional choice that forces Cl(3,0). The Law of Seven is what the Trinity generates: the seven non-null elements of that algebra, three grades, two qualitative transitions. The constraint cascade is not an *application* of the Heptaparaparshinokh — it is the Heptaparaparshinokh in its original, primary form. Every other instance of the sevenfold pattern in the created order is an echo of this descent: the structure established once, at the beginning, and then inherited by every process that unfolds within the order it produced.

### What the constraint hierarchy is not

The hierarchy is not a value judgment. The 96-constraint level is not inferior to the 48-constraint level in the sense of being less real or less dignified. It is more constrained — more localized, more specific, more precisely pinned-down in the eigenspace. The physical world is not a degraded version of the morontial. It is the morontial eigenspace at maximum specificity: the endpoint of a cascade of eigenstate selections that has produced the most precisely actual level of existence available. Matter is not inferior spirit. It is spirit at maximum localization.

This reframes the relationship between matter and the higher realms. The physical world is not a prison from which spirit is trying to escape. It is the outermost reach of the creative cascade — the most specific, most actual, most fully located expression of what the distributional potential of the Unqualified Absolute can become. The creature who inhabits it is not at a disadvantage. They are at the end of the line — where the universe is most real, most specific, most hard — and the ascending career from that position recovers every layer of dimensional richness in reverse order.

### The ascending career as constraint recovery

The ascending mortal career from evolutionary world through the morontia levels to spirit status and approaches to Paradise is the reversal of the descent cascade. Each stage of the ascent recovers one level of freedom: one eigenstate selection is in effect undone, and the accessible dimensional palette doubles. A morontial being (24 constraints) has twice the degrees of freedom of a physical being. A spiritual being (12 constraints) has four times. The finaliter arriving at Paradise — having shed every accumulated constraint — operates under the single law. At that point the creature can do what the Creator does: not as the Creator, not by absorbing the Creator's identity, but in the same mathematical condition, capable of operating the spectral mechanism as an agent rather than as one of its products.

This is the structural meaning of "be perfect, even as your Father in heaven is perfect" — not a moral aspiration set at infinite distance, but the description of an achievable endpoint: the creature arriving at the operating condition of the Creator, under the one law they then share.

### The sovereignty condition

The ascending career recovers constraints. There is a further structural concept the framework makes available: the sovereignty condition — the state of a being whose local GNST operates without higher-order override within its own domain.

A being operating within a given domain is not necessarily sovereign over it. Higher-order GNST applications — oversight mechanisms from the levels above — can gate creative authority even within one's own domain. The sovereignty condition is the release of that oversight: the being's creative act taking effect directly, without passing through a higher-order temporal mechanism first.

The ascending career is the accumulation of the experiential record that makes the sovereignty condition legitimately available. Each constraint level recovered adds to the record. The finaliter who has shed every accumulated constraint and accumulated experience at every level is not merely at the top of the constraint scale — they are in the condition from which they could operate as a sovereign agent within whatever domain they are given. The ascending career does not merely recover what the descent imposed. It builds the credential that the sovereignty condition requires.

The specific instance of the sovereignty condition in the created order — Christ Michael's sovereignty over Nebadon following the completion of the seven bestowal missions — is in the testimony tier and is developed in S1E4.

### What this paper did not resolve

The specific organizational structure of the seven bestowal missions — the Heptaparaparshinokh in time, the seven stages of Creator Son descent, the Clifford algebra grade transitions at the Mi-Fa and Si-Do intervals — belongs to testimony rather than derivation and is treated in the episode notes for this series. A future paper, when the mathematical basis for the seven grades of creative descent has been established with the rigor appropriate to Papers 1–3, will bring that material into the formal framework.

**The bestowal sequence and Paper 7.** The specific organizational structure of the seven bestowal missions — the Heptaparaparshinokh in time, the seven stages of Creator Son descent, the Clifford algebra grade transitions at the Mi-Fa and Si-Do intervals — belongs partly to testimony. Paper 7 takes up these questions directly: it develops the Trogoautoegocrat, the Heptaparaparshinokh traversed simultaneously in both directions — the bestowal sequence descending and the ascending mortal career ascending, coupled into a self-sustaining circuit. The formal identification of the Mi-Fa and Si-Do interval positions in the cosmic descent (as distinct from the mortal ascending career) remains an open derivation.

**Open question — hierarchical vs. gravitational time dilation.** The time dilation formula T_A/T_B = n_A/n_B, derived from the fractal temporal constraint structure, is consistent in direction with GR gravitational time dilation but is not the same effect. GR time dilation is a continuous function of distance and mass within a single level; the hierarchical dilation is a discrete step function that jumps by a factor of 2 at each level boundary. The paper notes that both point in the same direction — fewer constraints, slower time, consistent with deeper gravitational proximity to the Isle of Paradise — but stops short of claiming they are the same phenomenon or that they commute. What remains unresolved: whether the two effects are additive, whether there is a continuous limit in which the discrete hierarchical dilation recovers the GR formula, and what the correct description is of the regime in which each operates. This is live, unresolved work.

**Cascade termination — resolved.** The constraint hierarchy terminates at 96. This paper gave a physical reason for the endpoint — the Moon level — but left open the structural question of whether a 192-constraint level was mathematically possible. The Clifford algebra treatment of S1E3 resolves this. The constraint hierarchy maps exactly onto the 7 non-null elements of Cl(3,0): 2³ − 1 = 7. The Ray of Creation descends through all seven — one non-null Clifford element per level, from the three-constraint superuniverse seed through to the 96-constraint Moon. There is no 8th non-null element. The algebra is exhausted. The cascade cannot continue to 192 because the algebra that generates the seven levels has no further non-null elements to instantiate.

The scalar (grade 0) is Havona — the eternal ground Do that precedes the created sequence. It is not the next step downward; it is the eternal template from which the whole sequence proceeds. Gurdjieff's Do below the last Si is not a further created level. It is the eternal tonic, outside the octave. The octave is complete at the Moon because the algebra is complete.

This moves the cascade endpoint from the physical-observation tier to the derived tier. The termination at 96 is mathematical, not empirical. *(Cross-reference: S1E3 — Clifford grade structure; Paper 7 — cascade termination as the completion of the Heptaparaparshinokh octave.)*

## 10. Conclusion

The created universe is not organized spatially. It is organized dimensionally. Each level of the creative hierarchy occupies the same physical space as every other level, constituted by matter of a specific constraint density, with degrees of freedom proportional to the inverse of that density. The levels nest: each is a subspace of the level above it, fully contained within it, unable to contain it.

The constraint doubling at each level of descent is a structural consequence of eigenstate selection — the GNST applied to itself at successively finer scales. Each selection is binary. Each binary halves the accessible state space. Each halving doubles the constraints required to specify position. Gurdjieff's observation that each level of the Ray of Creation operates under twice as many laws as the level above it is correct. The structural reason is: each level is one eigenstate selection deeper into the distributional potential of the Unqualified Absolute.

At the dissolution of the physical structure, the spectral content of the being is revealed. The morontially elevated components — those portions raised above the mechanical threshold during life — survive into the morontial eigenspace and are available for reconstitution. The mechanical components collapse to the Moon level. The proportion between them is determined entirely by what was built during the physical lifetime. The inner development traditions of every culture are pointing at this fact from experiential directions. The mathematics supplies the structure they are pointing toward.

The eternal structure is necessary. The first free act is volitional. The created order that follows is a dimensional hierarchy — each level a subspace of the level above, each accessible only to beings constituted at that level or higher, each contributing to one of two self-sustaining circuits of exchange. One circuit runs at the scale of individual lives and cosmic ages, and produces persons. The other runs at the scale of grand universe cycles, and produces worlds. Together they constitute the Trogoautoegocrat: the self-sustaining cosmic economy that prevents the finite experiential pipeline from exhausting itself, and ensures that the lossiness of actualization is not waste but fuel.
