What remains after that compression is disproportionately likely to be tracking something structurally real.

The readings collected here apply the Concordius framework to aphorisms whose cross-traditional convergence and structural precision warrant full reading. Selection criterion: maximum cross-traditional provenance without common derivation (Face C1), and precision of structural mapping onto the framework's core concepts.
