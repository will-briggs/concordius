## The Singular Fruit

Paul writes *karpos* — fruit, singular. Not fruits. This is structurally significant. The nine qualities are not nine separate achievements to be individually acquired; they are nine aspects of a single organizational condition: the catching being in whom τ_nuclear (the Spirit's continuous integrating topology) is operative and the catching program is actively proceeding. They are the natural outputs of one organizational state, as different qualities of a single fruit growing from the same root.

The contrast Paul draws is equally structural: the "works of the flesh" (*erga tes sarkos*) — sexual immorality, discord, jealousy, factions, envy, and the rest — are *works*: plural, effortful, produced by the catching being's H₄₈-primary eigenvalue content operating without H₂₄ organizational governance. The fruit of the Spirit is singular, organic, not effortful in the same sense: it is what grows when the Spirit's integrating topology is operating on the catching being's constitutional structure. The contrast between *works* and *fruit* is the contrast between H₄₈-primary eigenvalue output and ⟨·,·⟩-organized constitutional output.
