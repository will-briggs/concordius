# A Structural Reading of Spectral Events

The readings in this section apply the Concordius framework to phenomena involving organizational content crossing or becoming visible across constraint levels — events at the H₂₄/H₄₈ boundary, or involving the H₂₄ organizational state of a being in unusual or concentrated expression at H₄₈.

These are not supernatural events in a category apart from the framework's structural account. They are structural events: instances of the cross-term mechanism operating at amplitude sufficient to produce observable H₄₈ effects, or of H₂₄ organizational states persisting or manifesting in ways the framework can account for precisely. The framework does not explain them away; it explains them.
