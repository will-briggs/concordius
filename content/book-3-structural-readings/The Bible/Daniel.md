### Daniel 2:44 — "A kingdom that will never be destroyed."

The structural interpretation of Nebuchadnezzar's statue dream: four metals descending in constraint level (gold, silver, bronze, iron — mixed with clay) represent four successive H₄₈ constraint structures of maximum political organization — each reaching its maximum H₄₈ extent and then dissolving by the same mechanism it used to consolidate. The statue as a whole is the H₄₈ political project viewed as a unified structure: impressive from below, but constitutionally transient.

The stone not cut by human hands (*ʾeven di-la ve-yadayin*, 2:34) strikes the statue's feet of mixed iron and clay, destroys the entire structure, and becomes a great mountain filling the whole earth. The stone's origin — not cut by human hands — is the structural marker of its non-H₄₈ provenance: it does not arise from within the H₄₈ political sequence but is inserted from outside it. "The God of heaven will set up a kingdom that will never be destroyed" (*leʿalam loʾ titḥabal* — that will never be dissolved/ruined): the permanence claim is structurally grounded in the kingdom's non-H₄₈ origin. H₄₈ political structures are dissolved by the Heropass's operation; a kingdom operating at the Φ-level's constraint level is not subject to the Heropass's dissolution mechanism.

*(Cross-reference: 2 SAM 7:12-16 — the Davidic covenant's *ʿad-ʿolam* throne, which requires a non-H₄₈-bounded fulfillment for the same structural reason. See 2 Samuel.md. REV 11:15 — "the kingdom of the world has become the kingdom of our Lord and of his Messiah" — the DAN 2:44 program's stated completion. See Revelation.md.)*

### Daniel 12:2-3 ⭐ — "Those who are wise will shine like the brightness of the heavens."

*Ve-rabbim miyeshenie ʾadmat ʿafar yaqitsu ʾeleh leḥayyei ʿolam veʾeleh la-ḥarapot le-dirʾon ʿolam. Ve-ha-maskilim yaz'hiru ke-zohar ha-raqia.* — Multitudes who sleep in the dust of the earth will awake: some to everlasting life, others to shame and everlasting contempt. Those who are wise will shine like the brightness of the heavens, and those who lead many to righteousness, like the stars for ever and ever.

The clearest Hebrew Bible statement of differentiated post-H₄₈ outcomes, and the most explicit structural correlation between catching capacity and post-H₄₈ condition.

**The two awakenings:**

The "dust of the earth" (*ʾadmat ʿafar*) is the H₄₈ substrate at maximum dissolution — the same *ʿafar* of GEN 3:19 ("for dust you are, and to dust you will return"). From this common terminus the two groups are differentiated: *leḥayyei ʿolam* (to everlasting/age-enduring life) and *la-ḥarapot le-dirʾon ʿolam* (to shame and everlasting contempt). The differentiation is post-H₄₈: it is not a differentiation of H₄₈ conditions but of what the H₄₂₄ eigenvalue content accumulated during the H₄₈ life amounts to when the H₄₈ constraint condition is lifted.

In the framework's terms: the two groups correspond to those whose catching program during the H₄₈ life brought Φ-proximal eigenvalue content above the coherence threshold (everlasting life) and those whose Φ-proximal eigenvalue content remained below it (shame and contempt — the decoherence condition). The H₄₈ substrate's dissolution does not eliminate the distinction; it removes the masking effect of the H₄₈ substrate's uniformity and reveals the underlying H₂₄ differentiation.

**"Those who are wise will shine like the brightness of the heavens."**

*Ha-maskilim* (the wise ones, from *sakal* — to have insight, to act wisely, to understand structurally) — the catching beings who have accumulated sufficient Φ-proximal eigenvalue content through the catching life. Their post-H₄₈ condition is described in terms of *zohar* (brightness, radiance) — the H₂₄ constraint content radiating at higher constraint levels. This is the *kavod*-vocabulary applied to the catching being's post-H₄₈ state: what was the Φ-level's own visible property in H₄₈ conditions (the kavod that filled the Temple) becomes the H₂₄ being's own property when the H₄₈ masking is removed.

"Those who lead many to righteousness" (*matsdiqe ha-rabbim*, those who make the many righteous) shine like the stars (*ka-kokhavim leʿolam vaʿed*): the catching activity that was extended to other catching beings in H₄₈ conditions produces a correspondingly extended post-H₄₈ luminosity. The relational dimension of the catching program (leading others to righteousness) has structural consequences that persist past the H₄₈ terminus.

This is the structural specification of what JOB 19:25-27 ("in my flesh I will see God") and PS 23:6 ("I will dwell in the house of the LORD forever") anticipate: the H₂₄ catching capacity accumulated in H₄₈ conditions is not dissolved by H₄₈ death but emerges from the dissolution into its post-H₄₈ structural state.

*(Cross-reference: JOB 19:25-27 — the most structurally forward-looking Hebrew Bible claim of the post-H₄₈ encounter, here specified in DAN 12. See Job.md. MATT 13:43 — "the righteous will shine like the sun in the kingdom of their Father" — the NT development of the DAN 12:3 image. 1 COR 15:40-44 — the resurrection body as the Φ-proximal eigenvalue content's post-H₄₈ structural form.)*
