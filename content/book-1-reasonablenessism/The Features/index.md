# The Features

---

*Features 1–12: no source is axiom and all are evidence; the steelman as the test of what survives; personal experience as testimony, not proof; the stance held to its own standard; provenance set aside as irrelevant to truth-value; the convergence of independent witnesses; the self-sealing claim whose every denial performs it; universal subjective convergence as grounds for inferring an objective fact; confidence labelled by how a thing is known; logic carried as far as it reaches and concordance named where it stops; the account requiring the fewest unexplained events preferred; and the measure of a thing by its fruit rather than its lineage.*

Reasonablenessism is not a doctrine to be believed but a discipline to be practised, and the discipline is these twelve features. Each is a single rule the Reasonable Person applies when weighing whatever presents itself as knowledge — plain enough to state in a sentence, demanding enough to take a lifetime to keep.

They are not a list of independent tips. They are organised by the Law of Three — the three forces any genuine act of judgment must mediate. The first four **discipline the Passive**: they train the evaluating mind to receive what comes to it without distorting it — every source treated as a witness, the opposing case stated at its strongest, personal experience weighed as testimony, and the stance itself held to its own standard. The next four **protect the Active**: they keep genuine signal from being lost to that same mind's distortions — provenance set aside, independent convergence counted, the self-sealing claim recognised, universal subjective agreement read for what it implies. The last four **constitute the Reconciling**: the working mechanisms that mediate between reality and the mind weighing it — confidence labelled by how it was earned, logic carried to its edge and concordance named past it, the fewest unexplained events preferred, and the whole judged by its fruit rather than its lineage.

Each feature has its own page, kept to the same registers throughout: the principle as a maxim, a plain-language account, a worked example from ordinary life, and — for those who want it — its grounding in the Concordius framework. The framing all of this rests on lives one level up, on the [[Book 1 - Reasonablenessism/index|book's index page]]: what the stance is, why the four great epistemologies fail at the edges, and who the Reasonable Person is. Here are the twelve features themselves.

## Summary of Contents

*Discipline the Passive — features that train the evaluating mind to receive properly.*

**[[Feature 1 - No Source Is Axiom|Feature 1 — No Source Is Axiom]]**
*No source is axiom. All are evidence. Stand on what you must — but state it plainly, and never mistake it for the ground.*

**[[Feature 2 - The Steelman Is the Test|Feature 2 — The Steelman Is the Test]]**
*Steelman first. Test what survives.*

**[[Feature 3 - Personal Experience as Testimony|Feature 3 — Personal Experience as Testimony]]**
*Personal experience is evidence. It is not proof.*

**[[Feature 4 - The Stance Applies to Itself|Feature 4 — The Stance Applies to Itself]]**
*Reasonablenessism applies to itself. No feature is exempt.*

*Protect the Active — features that ensure genuine signal reaches through Passive distortion.*

**[[Feature 5 - Provenance Is Irrelevant to Truth-Value|Feature 5 — Provenance Is Irrelevant to Truth-Value]]**
*Where something comes from does not determine whether it is true.*

**[[Feature 6 - Convergence of Independent Witnesses|Feature 6 — Convergence of Independent Witnesses]]**
*Independent convergence accumulates.*

**[[Feature 7 - The Self-Sealing Test|Feature 7 — The Self-Sealing Test]]**
*If every possible denial of a claim performs what it denies, the claim approaches axiom status.*

**[[Feature 8 - Universal Subjective Convergence Implies Objectivity|Feature 8 — Universal Subjective Convergence Implies Objectivity]]**
*When everyone who has ever looked confirms the same inner experience, that is the strongest available grounds for inferring an objective fact.*

*Constitute the Reconciling — the mechanisms that mediate between reality and the evaluating mind.*

**[[Feature 9 - Explicit Confidence Tiers|Feature 9 — Explicit Confidence Tiers]]**
*Label what you know by how you know it.*

**[[Feature 10 - Logic Where It Reaches; Concordance Where It Doesn't|Feature 10 — Logic Where It Reaches; Concordance Where It Doesn't]]**
*Follow the argument as far as it goes. When it stops, name where it stops and shift methods.*

**[[Feature 11 - Minimum Necessary Miracles|Feature 11 — Minimum Necessary Miracles]]**
*When comparing accounts, prefer the one requiring fewer unexplained events.*

**[[Feature 12 - Fruit, Not Lineage|Feature 12 — Fruit, Not Lineage]]**
*Evaluate by what it produces, not where it came from.*
